Lazy Reader

Lazy Reader is a hybrid of on device and online ai-model assisted reading application designed for books, PDFs, essays, research papers, and long-form articles. Instead of simply displaying pages, it transforms documents into a cleaner, more readable experience while providing intelligent reading and listening tools.

Features

📖 Reader-Optimized Layout

- Converts PDFs into a comfortable reading format.
- Book-like typography and spacing inspired by modern reading platforms.
- Designed for long reading sessions with reduced visual clutter.

🎙️ Intelligent Voice Reading

- High-quality text-to-speech playback.
- Adjustable reading speed.
- Reading progress tracking.
- Designed for audiobook-style consumption of books and documents.

🎯 Focus Mode

- Reads documents through a dedicated text overlay.
- Highlights the currently spoken content.
- Helps users follow along while listening.
- Improves concentration and reading comprehension.

🔍 Smart Word Lookup

- Double-tap any word to instantly access:
  - Meaning
  - Simplified explanation
  - Synonyms
  - Google search

🤖 Gemini-Powered Assistance

- Gemini integration for vocabulary explanations and language understanding.
- Helps readers understand difficult words and phrases without leaving the document.

🧹 Smart Text Cleaning

- Automatically detects:
  - Questions
  - Sidenotes
  - Symbols
  - Emojis
- Optional controls allow users to skip or remove distractions during reading and narration.

💾 Reading Progress

- Save options.
- Resume from where you left off.
- Navigate by pages or reading blocks.

🌙 Built for Deep Reading

- Dark mode support.
- Minimal distraction interface.
- Optimized for books, essays, philosophy, literature, and research material.
